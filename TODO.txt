Project description:
mvee is an application to sell or trade items of aproximately the same value, within Chihuahua Chihuahua Mexico.
Idealy, you would post your own Item, and wait for offers to either trade or sell your item. 
	Items will have the following atributes:
		-Name
		-Owner: ID
		-Brief description
		-Item type or category: Electronics, tools, clothing, etc
		-Descriptive image or set of images
		-Location: Comprised of LAT and LON		
		-Asking price, estimated price or estimated value
		-Accepts trades?
